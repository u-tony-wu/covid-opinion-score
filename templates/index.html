What's opinion score?

The opinion score produced by this app measures the extent to which a tweet advocates for practicing COVID health measures (such as social distancing, wearing a mask, or staying at home) and calls people to action, ranging from -1.0(against) to 1.0(for). Note that a score of zero means the tweet is neutral or ambiguous about COVID health measures, or is irrelevant to COVID-19.
